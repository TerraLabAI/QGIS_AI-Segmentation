    - Fully automatic: AI model and dependencies install themselves
    - Precise: positive and negative click points control boundaries
    - Flexible: Simple mode or Batch mode for multi-polygon export
    - Refinable: expand, contract, simplify, fill holes, remove small regions
    - Fast: auto GPU detection (CUDA, Apple Metal) with CPU fallback
    - Universal: Windows, macOS, Linux
    - Multilingual: English, French, Portuguese, Spanish

    Works with drone, satellite, aerial, or any raster imagery.
